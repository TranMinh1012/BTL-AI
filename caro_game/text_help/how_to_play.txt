When you click play in Main Menu,
GUI "Are you ready?" displays, 
you can choose Yes to start the game, or choose No to return Main Menu. 
If you click Yes, the game's GUI will display:
Press b to back to GUI"Are you ready?"
When playing, you can press 0 or 1 to select AI level (default is 1)
Rules of game is:
1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
2. You are X, AI is O.
Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
3. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row is the winner.
4. When all 9 squares are full, the game ends in a tie.
